Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Our Commitment: Your privacy is our priority. Camera data is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our services.

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Camera/Video data: All pose detection is processed on-device using MediaPipe. No images or video are ever recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Precise location: We do not collect GPS or location data.
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts list.
  • Browsing history: We do not track your web browsing.
  • Financial information: All payments are handled by Apple. We never see your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalized workout programs based on your goals, level, and preferences.
  • Track your workout progress and provide statistics.
  • Power the gamification system (XP, levels, badges, streaks).
  • Enable social features (friend lists, duels, leaderboards) if you opt in.
  • Send workout reminders and notifications (with your permission).
  • Improve the app through anonymized usage analytics.
  • Provide customer support when requested.

3. Camera Usage

Camera Privacy: This section is especially important as our app uses your camera for exercise form analysis.
  • The camera is used exclusively for real-time pose detection during exercises.
  • All processing happens on your device using Google's MediaPipe framework.
  • No images, video frames, or camera data are ever stored on your device or transmitted to any server.
  • The camera is only active when you explicitly start an exercise session.
  • You can revoke camera permission at any time through your device Settings.

4. HealthKit Integration

  • With your explicit permission, we may read and write workout data to Apple Health.
  • HealthKit data is handled according to Apple's strict privacy guidelines.
  • We only access the specific data types you authorize (workout sessions, active energy burned).
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.
  • You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time through Settings > Health > Data Access.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • On-Device: Workout data, preferences, and personal information are stored locally using SwiftData and UserDefaults.
  • Cloud (Subscribers): Plus and Premium subscribers' data may be synced via Firebase Firestore with encryption in transit and at rest.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
  • Firebase services are hosted on Google Cloud Platform with SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliance.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.
  • On-device data is removed when you uninstall the app.
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 14 months.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, KVKK (Turkish data protection law), and other applicable regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Data Portability: Export your workout data (CSV export available for Plus/Premium).
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke permissions (camera, HealthKit, notifications) at any time.
  • Object: Object to processing of your data for specific purposes.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@newgentracker.app.

9. Children's Privacy

  • The App is not intended for children under 14.
  • Users aged 14-17 must have parental or guardian consent.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14.
  • If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 14, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence (including the United States) through our use of Firebase/Google Cloud services. These transfers are protected by standard contractual clauses and Google's data processing agreements.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.
